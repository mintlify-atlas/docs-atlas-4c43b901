PineconeMC is a Minecraft launcher built on Prism Launcher that adds first-class support for [Ely.by](https://ely.by) accounts. Unlike other forks, PineconeMC uses OAuth2 — your credentials never touch the launcher — and applies official Ely.by authlib patches so your skin shows up everywhere, even on servers without special plugins.

## Why PineconeMC?

PineconeMC goes beyond simply adding a username/password prompt for Ely.by. It implements the full OAuth2 flow and uses Ely.by's official authlib patches rather than the community authlib-injector alone.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download PineconeMC">
    Grab the latest release from the [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ElyPrismLauncher/Launcher/releases/latest) page for your operating system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your account">
    Open **Accounts** in the top-right corner and choose **Add Ely.by Account** or **Add Microsoft Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an instance">
    Click the **Add Instance** button, select your Minecraft version, and optionally choose a mod loader like Fabric or Forge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play">
    Select your instance and click **Launch**. PineconeMC handles Java, authlib patches, and game arguments automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
